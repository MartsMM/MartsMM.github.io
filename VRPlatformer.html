Virtual Reality Platforming Game

About

  • Prototype of a VR platforming game made in 2 semesters for my final year dissertation project
  • Push and pull against the environment to control your movements and tackle obstacles
  • Made with C# in Unity

I really enjoyed working on this project, it gave me the opportunity to establish a variety of skills surrounding development. I had no experience making games when starting my dissertation. As a result, I have developed an ability to quickly become proficient with unfamiliar technologies.
